## Why

Coaches can generate multiple spotlight rounds per session, but there is no way to remove a round that was generated by mistake or is no longer needed. This leaves coaches unable to correct errors without manual database intervention.

## What Changes

- Add a `DeleteRound` operation to the spotlight service that removes a specific round and its pairings
- Expose a delete action on the session management page so coaches can remove a round from the UI
- After deletion, remaining rounds keep their original round numbers (no renumbering)

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `spotlight-round-deletion`: Ability to delete a generated spotlight round and its associated pairings from a session

### Modified Capabilities
- `multiple-rounds-per-session`: Round numbers are not renumbered after deletion; gaps in numbering are acceptable

## Impact

- `Peers.Spotlights`: New `DeleteRound` method on `ISpotlightService` and its implementation
- `Peers.Web`: Delete action/handler on the session Manage page
- `Peers.Persistence`: Cascade delete configuration for spotlight round -> pairings (verify existing config)
- `Peers.Spotlights.Tests`: New tests for deletion behavior
